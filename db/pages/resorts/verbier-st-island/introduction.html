Verbier Ski Holidays Skiing & snowboarding in Verbier, 4 Valleys, Valais

Overview



Ski holidays in Verbier - a famous, international and cosmopolitan ski resort in the heart of the largest ski area in Switzerland - The 4 Valleys & Ski St-Bernard.


The skiing in and around Verbier is amazing and caters for all levels of skier and boarder especially for the more advanced who will relish the chance to ski or board some of the high mountain trails around Mont Fort - absolutely fantastic.


Spectacular scenery and challenging skiing to say the least! For family fun or an action-packed break, Verbier caters for everyone both on and off the slopes.

Verbier Ski Holiday Rentals


In terms of the ski accommodation in Verbier, there is a myth that the prices are in the stratosphere. Whilst there are luxurious ski chalets and apartments that are very expensive, there are many places that wont break the bank.


Wherever you choose to stay in Valais, whether a holiday rental in the Rhone Valley or in one of the charming ski resort, the scenery is spectacular and you are never far from the ski lifts and local apres-ski bars and restaurants. The other major benefit of renting a holiday home is that they provide the freedom, flexibility and space to do what you want, when you want - ideal for familes, groups and friends.

Our view on skiing in Verbier

We think Verbier is a fantastic ski resort with some superb skiing for all levels. Combined with a lively and sophisticated nigh life, it's difficult not to have a good time on a Verbier ski holiday.
