Skiing in Chamonix is all about exploring this huge area. The Chamonix piste map shows 400km of pistes spread across the various sectors from Les Houches at the entrance of the valley up to La Tour close to the Swiss border.

The highlight of ski holiday in Chamonix is the 24km Vallee Blanche glacial route starting at 3842m! Overall, Chamonix ski resort has every type of terrain possible from beginner to advanced and is a mecca for extreme skiing.

With the full lift pass, you can also ski in...

  • Argentiere
  • Les Houches
  • Courmayeur, through the Mont Blanc tunnel
  • Megeve, in the Evasion Mont Blanc carousel

For some more serious skiing adventure, you can always do some heli-skiing on the Italian side of Mont Blanc (banned in France) in Courmayeur, which is organised by Evolution 2.
